Who We Are

SpaceWalkers is a story-like blog providing marvelous tales of all things regarding the outer space and the universe. We aim to provide a memorable experience when you explore our page by explaining vividly our experiences while on space. Our mission is to encourage our future minds to become space travelers as well.
We are based in New Jersey. We post weekly.
We have been operating since 2002 and have managed to build up a reputable establishment online. We have a great team and aim to grow our personnel more and more, offering our readers the best detailed stories and experiences available.
If you spotted our subtle easter-egg, you are well on your way to becoming one of us ;)
